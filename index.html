What is the area of an inscribed quadrilateral calculator?
The Brahmagupta formula calculator is a tool that measures the area of inscribed quadrilateral when the lengths of all sides are known. How to calculate area of an inscribed quadrilateral?
To calculate area of an inscribed quadrilateral you should use the following formula:
area of an inscribed quadrilateral = ((p - length of quadrilateral side a) * (p - length of quadrilateral side b) * (p - length of quadrilateral side c) * (p - length of quadrilateral side d))^1/2
